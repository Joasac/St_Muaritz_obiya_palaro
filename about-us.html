About St. Moritz Catholic Church

Building communities through faith, service, and sustainable development since our founding

Our Story

St. Moritz Catholic Church Organization was established as a beacon of hope and service in the heart of Uganda. Founded on the principles of Catholic social teaching, we have dedicated ourselves to serving the spiritual, educational, health, and developmental needs of our community.

Our mission began with a small group of faithful believers who saw the need for comprehensive community development. Today, we operate multiple ministries that touch every aspect of community life - from healthcare and education to agriculture and youth development.

Through the grace of God and the dedication of our members, volunteers, and partners, we continue to grow and expand our impact, always staying true to our Catholic values and commitment to serving those in need.

Our Foundation

Our Mission

To build vibrant Catholic communities through integrated spiritual, educational, health, and developmental services that empower individuals and families to reach their full potential in Christ.

Our Vision

A transformed society where every person experiences God's love through access to quality healthcare, education, spiritual growth, and sustainable livelihood opportunities.

Faith

Rooted in Catholic teaching and guided by the Gospel values of love, compassion, and service to others, especially the poor and marginalized.

Integrity

Operating with transparency, accountability, and ethical practices in all our programs and interactions with the community.

Community

Building strong partnerships and fostering collaborative relationships that strengthen and uplift entire communities.

Sustainability

Promoting environmentally responsible practices and developing programs that create lasting positive change for future generations.
