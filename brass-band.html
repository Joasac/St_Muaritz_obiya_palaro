About Our Brass Band

The St. Moritz Brass Band has been an integral part of our church's worship and community life for over 12 years. Our talented musicians are dedicated to glorifying God through music while enriching our worship services and community events. We welcome musicians of all skill levels and provide training and instruments to those in need.

Worship Excellence

Enhancing worship services with inspiring and reverent musical performances

Performing at community celebrations, festivals, and special occasions

Our Musical Ensemble

Brass Section

The heart of our band featuring trumpets, trombones, and horns.

  • Trumpet players (6 members)
  • Trombone section (4 members)
  • French horn players (2 members)
  • Tuba/Bass section (2 members)

Percussion

Providing rhythm and energy to every performance.

  • Drum set and snare drums
  • Timpani and kettle drums
  • Cymbals and auxiliary percussion
  • Traditional African drums

String & Wind

Additional instruments that add richness to our sound.

  • Guitar and bass guitar
  • Flute and clarinet
  • Saxophone section
  • Keyboard and piano

Vocal Section

Beautiful voices that blend with our instrumental music.

  • Lead vocalists and soloists
  • Harmony and backup singers
  • Children's choir section
  • Traditional and contemporary styles

Our Music Ministry

Music Education Program

We offer comprehensive music education to develop new talent and improve existing skills.

  • Individual instrument lessons
  • Music theory and reading
  • Ensemble playing techniques
  • Performance skills development
  • Free instrument rental program
  • Youth music mentorship

Community Outreach

Our band regularly performs at community events and supports local celebrations.

  • School graduation ceremonies
  • Community festivals and parades
  • Cultural celebration events
  • Charity fundraising concerts
  • Inter-church musical collaborations
  • Public holiday performances

Worship Enhancement

Our primary mission is to enhance worship experiences through inspiring and reverent music.

  • Liturgical music for Mass
  • Processional and recessional music
  • Special season celebrations
  • Meditation and prayer music
  • Contemporary and traditional hymns
  • Multi-lingual worship songs

Band Achievements

Our band has achieved recognition for musical excellence and community service over the years.

  • 2023: Best Church Band - Regional Competition
  • 2022: Community Service Award
  • 2021: Youth Music Development Recognition
  • 12 years of continuous service
  • 50+ active members
  • 200+ performances annually

Join Our Musical Family

Whether you're an experienced musician or just starting your musical journey, we welcome you to join our brass band. We provide instruments, training, and a supportive community of fellow musicians.

Requirements to Join:

  • Age 12 years and above
  • Commitment to regular practice
  • Willingness to learn and improve
  • Respect for fellow musicians
  • Dedication to our ministry mission

What We Provide:

  • Free instrument rental
  • Professional music instruction
  • Sheet music and learning materials
  • Performance opportunities
  • Uniform and equipment
