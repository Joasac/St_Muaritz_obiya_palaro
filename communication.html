About Our ICT Department

The St. Moritz Communication & ICT Department is dedicated to bringing modern technology and digital literacy to our community. We provide essential ICT services, training programs, and communication support to help bridge the digital divide and prepare our community for the digital age.

Digital Literacy

Computer training and digital skills development for all age groups

Internet Access

Free WiFi and internet access for community members and students

Tech Support

Technical support and maintenance for community organizations
