About Our Education Sponsorship Program

The St. Moritz Education Sponsorship Program was established to break the cycle of poverty through education. We believe that every child deserves access to quality education regardless of their family's financial situation. Our program has been transforming lives for over 8 years, helping hundreds of students achieve their educational goals.

Financial Support

Covering school fees, uniforms, books, and other educational expenses

Mentorship

Pairing students with mentors for guidance and personal development

Progress Monitoring

Regular academic progress tracking and additional support when needed

Types of Educational Support

Primary Education

Complete support for primary school education from P1 to P7.

  • School fees and registration
  • Uniforms and shoes
  • Books and stationery
  • Exam fees and certificates

Cost: $150 per year

Secondary Education

Comprehensive support for secondary school from S1 to S6.

  • Tuition and boarding fees
  • School requirements and materials
  • Laboratory and library fees
  • National examination fees

Cost: $300-500 per year

Higher Education

University and tertiary institution scholarships for exceptional students.

  • University tuition fees
  • Accommodation and meals
  • Books and research materials
  • Academic conference attendance

Cost: $800-1,200 per year

Vocational Training

Technical and vocational training for practical skills development.

  • Technical institute fees
  • Workshop materials and tools
  • Certification and licensing
  • Job placement support

Cost: $200-400 per year

How Our Sponsorship Program Works

1. Student Identification

We identify needy students through community outreach, school visits, and applications from families and local leaders.

2. Assessment & Selection

Students are assessed based on academic performance, financial need, and commitment to education.

3. Sponsor Matching

We match students with individual sponsors or groups who commit to supporting their education journey.

4. Ongoing Support

Regular monitoring, progress reports, and additional support ensure student success and sponsor satisfaction.

Success Stories

Sarah's Journey to University

Sarah joined our program in P5 after her father passed away. Through sponsorship support, she completed her education and is now studying medicine at Makerere University.

"Without the sponsorship program, I would never have been able to continue my education. Now I'm studying to become a doctor to help my community." - Sarah Namukasa

Moses - From Sponsored Student to Sponsor

Moses received sponsorship support throughout secondary school and technical training. He now runs a successful electrical business and sponsors two students himself.

"The program didn't just pay for my education; it taught me the value of giving back. Now I help other students achieve their dreams." - Moses Kasule

Our Program Impact

Over 8 years, our sponsorship program has made a significant impact on education in our community.

  • 300+ students sponsored
  • 95% completion rate
  • 150+ graduates in employment
  • 25+ university graduates
  • 50+ technical graduates
  • 20+ former students now sponsoring others

Community Transformation

Our education sponsorship program has transformed entire families and communities through the power of education.

  • Reduced child labor in the community
  • Increased school enrollment rates
  • Improved community health outcomes
  • Enhanced economic opportunities
  • Stronger family stability
  • Greater community pride and development
