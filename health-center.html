About Our Health Center

The St. Moritz Health Center has been serving our community for over 15 years, providing quality healthcare services to people of all backgrounds. Our mission is to ensure that every person has access to quality healthcare regardless of their economic status.

Expert Medical Staff

Qualified doctors, nurses, and healthcare professionals committed to excellence

Modern Facilities

Well-equipped medical facility with modern diagnostic and treatment equipment

Compassionate Care

Patient-centered approach with emphasis on dignity, respect, and holistic healing

Our Medical Services

General Medicine

Comprehensive primary healthcare, routine check-ups, and treatment of common illnesses.

Maternal & Child Health

Prenatal care, delivery services, postnatal care, and pediatric healthcare services.

Immunization

Complete vaccination programs for children and adults following WHO guidelines.

Laboratory Services

Diagnostic tests including blood work, urine tests, and other laboratory investigations.

Pharmacy

Well-stocked pharmacy with affordable medications and proper pharmaceutical guidance.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency services for urgent medical situations and first aid treatment.

Community Health Programs

Health Education & Awareness

Regular community health education sessions covering topics like hygiene, nutrition, disease prevention, and healthy living practices.

  • Monthly health talks
  • Hygiene and sanitation workshops
  • Nutrition education programs
  • Disease prevention campaigns

Mobile Health Services

Our mobile health unit reaches remote areas, bringing healthcare services directly to communities that need them most.

  • Village health outreaches
  • Mobile vaccination campaigns
  • Health screenings in schools
  • Emergency response services

Chronic Disease Management

Specialized programs for managing chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and other long-term health conditions.

  • Diabetes management program
  • Hypertension monitoring
  • Regular follow-up appointments
  • Lifestyle counseling sessions

Mental Health Support

Recognizing the importance of mental health, we provide counseling and support services for emotional and psychological wellbeing.

  • Individual counseling sessions
  • Group therapy programs
  • Mental health awareness campaigns
  • Crisis intervention services
