About Our Community Library

The St. Moritz Community Library serves as a vital educational resource for our community, providing free access to books, digital resources, and learning spaces. Established in 2010, our library supports lifelong learning and literacy development for people of all ages and backgrounds.

Diverse Collection

Over 5,000 books covering fiction, non-fiction, textbooks, and reference materials

Digital Resources

Computer access, internet connectivity, and digital learning platforms

Community Space

Quiet study areas, reading rooms, and spaces for community programs
