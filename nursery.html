About Our Nursery School

St. Moritz Nursery School has been providing quality early childhood education for over 10 years. We believe that the early years are crucial for a child's development, and we strive to create a nurturing environment where children can grow academically, socially, emotionally, and spiritually.

Loving Care

Experienced caregivers providing individual attention to each child

Play-Based Learning

Educational activities through play, games, and interactive learning

Safe Environment

Secure, clean, and child-friendly facilities designed for young learners

Our Early Learning Programs

Baby Class (3-4 years)

Introduction to basic skills, social interaction, and routine establishment.

  • Basic motor skills development
  • Simple counting and colors
  • Potty training support
  • Social skills and sharing

Middle Class (4-5 years)

Building foundational academic skills and independence.

  • Pre-reading and writing skills
  • Number recognition and counting
  • Creative arts and crafts
  • Group activities and teamwork

Top Class (5-6 years)

Primary school preparation with advanced early learning skills.

  • Reading and writing preparation
  • Basic mathematics concepts
  • Science exploration activities
  • School readiness skills

Meal Program

Nutritious meals and snacks to support healthy growth and development.

  • Breakfast and lunch provided
  • Healthy snacks throughout the day
  • Special dietary accommodations
  • Nutrition education

Facilities & Special Activities

Learning Facilities

Our nursery is equipped with age-appropriate facilities designed specifically for young children.

  • Bright, colorful classrooms
  • Child-sized furniture and equipment
  • Educational toys and learning materials
  • Safe outdoor playground
  • Clean toilet and washing facilities
  • Dining hall for meals

Special Programs

We offer various special activities to enhance your child's learning experience and development.

  • Music and movement sessions
  • Art and craft activities
  • Nature walks and exploration
  • Cooking and life skills
  • Cultural celebrations
  • Field trips and excursions

Parent Involvement

We believe parents are partners in their child's education and encourage active participation.

  • Regular parent-teacher meetings
  • Open days and visiting hours
  • Home-school communication books
  • Parent volunteer opportunities
  • Parenting workshops and seminars
  • Progress reports and assessments

Health & Safety

The health and safety of our children is our top priority in everything we do.

  • Daily health checks
  • First aid trained staff
  • Clean and sanitized environment
  • Secure premises with controlled access
  • Emergency procedures in place
  • Nutritious meal preparation
