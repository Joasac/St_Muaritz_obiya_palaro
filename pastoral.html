Our Pastoral Care Ministry

At St. Moritz, we believe that every person deserves compassionate care and spiritual guidance through all of life's journeys. Our Pastoral Care Ministry is dedicated to walking alongside individuals and families during times of joy, sorrow, crisis, and celebration. We provide a listening ear, spiritual counsel, and practical support rooted in Christian love and understanding.

Spiritual Guidance

Biblical counseling and spiritual direction for personal growth and faith development

Emotional Support

Compassionate listening and emotional support during difficult times

Community Care

Connecting people with resources and building supportive community relationships

Our Pastoral Services

Individual Counseling

One-on-one pastoral counseling for personal, spiritual, and emotional challenges.

  • Personal spiritual direction
  • Crisis intervention and support
  • Grief and loss counseling
  • Addiction recovery support

Family Counseling

Supporting families through challenges and strengthening family relationships.

  • Marriage counseling and preparation
  • Family conflict resolution
  • Parenting guidance and support
  • Teen and youth family issues

Hospital & Home Visits

Bringing pastoral care directly to those who cannot come to us.

  • Hospital visitation ministry
  • Homebound and elderly care
  • Communion for the sick
  • Prayer and spiritual support

Life Celebrations

Pastoral support for significant life events and milestones.

  • Wedding preparation and ceremonies
  • Baptism and confirmation preparation
  • Funeral and memorial services
  • Blessing ceremonies

Support Groups

Group support and fellowship for people facing similar challenges.

  • Grief and bereavement support
  • Divorce recovery groups
  • Addiction recovery support
  • Chronic illness support

24/7 Crisis Support

Emergency pastoral care available around the clock for urgent situations.

  • Crisis intervention hotline
  • Emergency hospital visits
  • Immediate counseling support
  • Last rites and final blessing
