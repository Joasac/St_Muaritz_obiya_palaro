About St. Moritz Primary School

Established in 1998, St. Moritz Primary School has been a beacon of educational excellence in our community. We provide quality education rooted in Christian values, preparing students not only for academic success but also for moral and spiritual growth.

Qualified Teachers

Experienced and dedicated educators committed to student success

Modern Facilities

Well-equipped classrooms, library, laboratory, and playground facilities

Christian Values

Education grounded in faith, love, and moral principles

Academic Excellence

Core Subjects

Comprehensive curriculum covering Mathematics, English, Science, Social Studies, and Religious Education.

  • Mathematics & Problem Solving
  • English Language & Literature
  • Science & Nature Studies
  • Social Studies & Geography

Creative Arts

Developing creativity and artistic expression through various art forms and creative activities.

  • Visual Arts & Drawing
  • Music & Singing
  • Drama & Performance
  • Crafts & Handwork

Physical Education

Promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and healthy lifestyle through sports and physical activities.

  • Football & Netball
  • Athletics & Track Events
  • Traditional Games
  • Health & Fitness

Computer Studies

Introducing students to technology and digital literacy for the modern world.

  • Basic Computer Skills
  • Internet Safety
  • Educational Software
  • Digital Citizenship
