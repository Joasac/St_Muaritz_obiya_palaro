Our Vision

At St. Moritz Youth Ministry, we believe that young people are not just the church of tomorrow, but the church of today. We provide a safe, welcoming environment where youth aged 13-25 can explore their faith, develop leadership skills, and build lasting friendships.

Faith Formation

Growing closer to God through prayer, study, and community

Fellowship

Building authentic relationships and supporting one another

Service

Making a difference through community service and outreach
